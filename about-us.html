About Industrywala

India's trusted industrial valve distributor and hydraulic valves manufacturer committed to excellence since 2022

Our Story

Industrywala was started as a brainchild of the new generation, building upon over 50 years of family business experience in serving Indian industries[2][27]. Founded in 2022 by Husain Kapdawala, an internationally certified public speaker dedicated to helping factories run efficiently[15], we emerged with a clear vision to revolutionize industrial equipment supply.

From our strategic headquarters in Nagpur, Maharashtra, we have built an exceptional reputation as a reliable hydraulic valves manufacturer India and pneumatic equipment supplier. Our transition from a traditional brick-and-mortar store to a modern digital platform allows us to leverage our vast inventory strength while serving diverse industries including manufacturing, oil and gas, power plants, chemical processing, and water treatment[2].

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as the preferred valve distributor Gujarat, Mumbai, Delhi, and across India. We specialize in providing expertly engineered products and industrial automation consultation to meet the evolving needs of modern industries.
